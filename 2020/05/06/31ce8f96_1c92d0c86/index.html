20081213

Forward pleased at FIFA World Player 2008 nomination


Fernando Torres was grateful to colleagues as the striker was listed alongside four other players for the FIFA World Player award.“I want to thank my team-mates from club and country once again for all their help. Without their support and contribution it would have been impossible to have been considered for these individual prizes. I feel proud to have received recognition from national team managers and team captains from the world of the football. It’s a luxury just to have been included in this group of players. I am happy, and this serves for me to continue working hard each and every day so that I continue to receive praise.”


Fernando Torres and Liverpool’s goalscorer from the weekend’s clash at home to Hull have got on like a house on fire since the Spaniard landed on Merseyside. “From the first day we clicked out on the pitch. We found each other with ease. It is easy to play in the same team as Gerrard because he is a great player and makes everything easy for you out there. I have said this before, he is the best player I have played alongside. He is unbelievable and his consistency is worthy of admiration.”
