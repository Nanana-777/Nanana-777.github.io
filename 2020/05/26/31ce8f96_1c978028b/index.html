We meet up a lot outside of football
Created 2020-05-26| Updated 2020-05-26|Text

We have a great understanding both on and off the pitch. We are good friends, he's a good laugh, and a great person to have around. We just clicked. l love playing alongside him, and his movement and style of play are great to watch. He will be sorely missed. It’s a pleasure to see him in the Liverpool Red shirt, and when he is on form, he’s the best striker in the world. Perhaps Messi is the best all round player in the game at the moment, but I firmly believe that Torres is the best number '9' around. He’s our new Ian Rush. 场上场下我们都很了解对方。我们是好朋友，他是一个有趣优秀，值得拥有他在身边的人。我们一见如故。我喜欢在他身边踢球，他的跑位和球风赏心悦目。我会很想念他。看着他穿上利物浦的红色球衣是一种快乐，他在状态的时候，他是世上最佳前锋。可能梅西是现在的最佳球员，但我坚信托雷斯是最佳"九"号。他是我们的新伊恩拉什。虽然我们离对方居住的地方有45分钟的车程，我们常常在足球之外的地方聚会。我们待在梅尔伍德里花整天时间来制造笑话。

全篇都是糖！什么车程有45分钟还常常meet up，我四舍五入当date了。这篇糖在网络上就只剩下托蒸煮本人的官网存着了，表白一下托蒸煮下吧，很多糖也是从托蒸煮官网扒回来的，也是只有托蒸煮有把糖备份，我CP粉头是蒸煮本人。

Gerrard: "Torres will be sorely missed"

Steve Gerrard spoke to Spanish newspaper 'AS' ahead of the semifinal clash in Spain with Atletico Madrid. Gerrard, who is overjoyed at having the striker as a friend and a teammate at Liverpool, hopes his side can overcome the forward's absence over the two legged tie... and hinted that he would love to see Torres in an England shirt.

- The Torres-Gerrard combination has struck fear into defences home and abroad...how have you forged such a special partnership?

We have a great understanding both on and off the pitch. We are good friends, he's a good laugh, and a great person to have around. We just clicked. l love playing alongside him, and his movement and style of play are great to watch. He will be sorely missed. It’s a pleasure to see him in the Liverpool Red shirt, and when he is on form, he’s the best striker in the world. Perhaps Messi is the best all round player in the game at the moment, but I firmly believe that Torres is the best number '9' around. He’s our new Ian Rush. Although we live 45 minutes apart, we meet up a lot outside of football. We spend all day at Melwood cracking jokes.

- Torres was a favourite with fans almost from day one...

The fans want a striker who can score goals, but there is a lot more to it than that. The supporters like everything that Torres has to offer, his attitude, passion, and dedication. Since scoring against Chelsea in his Anfield debut. his raport with the fans has been excellent. If he remains at the club for many years to come, he will become a club legend.

- With the World Cup right around the corner... which player (non-English) would you like to have in your team?

Fernando Torres and Wayne Rooney would make for a very explosive strike partnership.
