Gerrard: “We haven't played there with Fernando Torres, have we?”

that Fernando Torres will be the difference between the two sides when they meet at Stamford Bridge next Wednesday. When asked about Liverpool’s poor goalscoring record at Stamford Bridge, Gerrard replied bluntly: “We haven't played there with Torres, have we?”

The current club top goalscorer came close to scoring on two occasions in the first-leg game at Anfield, but the Chelsea keeper pulled off two brilliant saves to deny the Spaniard. Had Fernando scored, it would have been his eighth goal in his last eight home games.

Gerrard will be looking forward to seeing Torres at Stamford Bridge. The striker was absent from both of Liverpool's trips to Chelsea this season, in the Premier League and Carling Cup, but scored his first goal for the club against in the Blues at the start of the season.

The Merseysider feels that his team does not need any extra-motivation: “We know this tie is not over. It's the second leg of the European Cup semi-final – if you can't motivate yourself for that you shouldn't be playing football”.
