Torres: I Prefer Gerrard To Lampard
Created 2020-04-25| Updated 2020-04-25|Text

“I liked him even before I came to Anfield and it is amazing to play at his side.”

Fernando Torres is a big fan of Liverpool's skipper, Steven Gerrard.

Liverpool forward Fernando Torres has piled praise on the Red's skipper, Steven Gerrard, and insisted he would rather have him in his side than Chelsea's Frank Lampard.

 
The Spaniard was speaking ahead of today's clash between the two sides at Anfield, which has become a must-win game following Manchester United's 1-0 win over Everton yesterday. 
 
Speaking to the News of the World, Torres revealed how highly he rates Gerrard and insisted he is one of the best midfielders in the world. 
 
“ For me Gerrard is an idol,” he said. 
 
“ I liked him even before I came to Anfield and it is amazing to play at his side. 
 
“ He is on my list of the best midfielders in the world, no doubt. He has a great shot, he arrives in the penalty box at the right time and has great vision on the pitch. 
 
 “At times he is almost another striker. A midfielder who scores and sets up goals like he does is something special.” 
 
Of course Gerrard and Lampard play alongside each other internationally, for England, but neither have been able to consistently reproduce their club form for the Three Lions which has often led to debates as to who is the better player. 
 
Torres is clearly in no doubt and admitted that whilst the two players are quite similar, he would rather play with Gerrard. 
 
“Frank Lampard is a great footballer too, of course. He has many aspects of his game that are similar to Gerrard," he said
 
 “But if you ask me which player I would rather have at Liverpool I will say Stevie. He is a key player for me. He is truly incredible.

“He has a great connection with the fans and is such a big part of the club.” 
 
Famously, Gerrard almost joined Lampard at Chelsea back in 2005 before changing his mind at the last minute and deciding to remain at Anfield.
