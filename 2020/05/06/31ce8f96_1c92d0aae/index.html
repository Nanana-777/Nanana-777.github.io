Steven is the best player I have ever played
Created 2020-05-06| Updated 2020-05-06|Text

16. MairaF9T (19:56 h)Fernando, what is it like to play alongside great players likes for example Steven Gerrard? 

Steven is the best player I have ever played alongside with. He is a natural born leader, a great footballer, a great captain... and a amazing person. I am learning a lot from him.

1. MªCarmen_bcn (18:49 h)
Before you arrived at Liverpool, the club had quite an important fan base in Spain due to the number of Spanish players playing for them. Do you think that your signing for the Reds has meant that more Spanish people now follow the Premier League? and that Liverpool is considered by many as their second team?

"First off I would like to say a big hello to everyone who has joined the Chat, thanks! I will try to reply to the maximum number of questions possible. I would love for Liverpool to be seen by people in Spain as their second team. I feel that Rafa Benitez’s arrival, along with players like Morientes, Luis García, Xabi or Pepe Reina, has led to an increased interest in the club back home. The Premiership is also a great competition with a large following back in Spain. Also, before joining Liverpool, I used to watch quite a lot of matches from the Premier League."

2. Rowan (18:50 h)
My name is Rowan and I am writing to you from Jordan. Have your teammates congratulated you on your great start and on the way that you have adapted to the change? By the way, you are my idol! Lots of kisses xxx

"All my team-mates have treated me really well. Everyone has been great from day one. For example like when Gerrard and Carragher sent me messages to my phone welcoming me to the club. And also all the people who work at the club, from here I would like to thank them all for their time and help. And also Pepe Reina and Xabi, as they help me out with any problems or doubts that I may have understanding the language."

3. *mamen* (sevilla) (18:54 h)
Do you think that you can finish the season as top goalscorer ? and if you did, how would you feel?

"I would be really happy, but at the end of the day the team comes first. A team success will always be more important than any individual prize or award. Though it is true that the side which wins the league, also has the leagues top goalscorer in its team. I have to admit that it would be really nice to finish up as the top goalscorer, but it is more important that Liverpool do really well this season."

4. Merxe (18:58 h)
How do you feel knowing that the Atletico Madrid fans this season are not missing you?

"I am not too sure that I see things the same way as you do Merxe. Whenever I have returned to Spain, the fans have given me a very warm welcome. I have always been treated well, almost as if I had never left. Though it would be great if this was true because it would be a positive sign and would mean that Atletico are doing really well. "

5. alvin (19:02 h)

"I have signed a six year contract and I hope that all goes well. I would like to have a long and successful career at Anfield. Ian Rush is a legend and I am still a long way away from his amazing feats. "

6. Janice (19:13 h)

"I would not say since playing for Liverpool, as I am still only 23, and I feel that I am still progressing as a footballer."

7. Lissie (19:17 h)

8. Maria (19:23 h)

9. Mr L Adams (19:26 h)
Hi there Fernando, how is life away from your close personal friends? Have they visted you in Liverpool as of yet? Gracias .

"I am happy in Liverpool. I like the little that I have seen of the city, and also the people who live here and the way that they love the game. My friends are regular visitors and I have not had time to miss anything from back in Spain."

10. rocio_9 (19:32 h)
Hello Fernando! Thanx for spending time with us again! I have a quick question... what do you have to say to those Atletico Madrid fans who say that you left the club for more money? Or those that have even labelled you a traitor?!? Thanx and good luck!

"I am not a traitor and I did not leave because of money. If that had been the case, I would have left earlier because there we received higher offers in the past. As I have said before, the Liverpool project looked good and I liked what I saw... so I thought that it was the right time for me to leave Atletico Madird. I had been at the club since I was 11 years old and I needed a new challenge. It was a tough decision but I made the right choice.”

12. rakel_ct9 (19:39 h)

"I had been told long before signing that the club is a living legend, with plenty of history, and good club to be at, both as a footballer and a person... and to tell the truth everyone has been true. This is truly a great club."

20. El Kid (20:20 h)

21. Bazza76 (20:24 h)

"The first thing the club did when I arrived was to hand me a DVD along with two books about the history of Liverpool. Shankly and Paisley were two great managers, and are both legends... and they are respected and loved by all of our fans. I have also been told several great stories about the both of them. As for the strikers, I would have to choose Kenny Dalglish, Robbie Fowler and Ian Rush."
